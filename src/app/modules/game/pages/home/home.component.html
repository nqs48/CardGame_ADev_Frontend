STEP 1

The game consists of randomly dealing 5 cards to the 6 players or to each previously created player.

STEP 2

The game consists of each player betting their cards and the one who has all the cards wins the game..

STEP 3

Each player bets based on their deck of cards and the card with the most XP wins the game.

STEP 4

After all the players put their bet card, the card that adds the most points wins, it means that the player who has the best card takes the other cards that are on the board (it is like a conventional betting system).

FINISH

The game ends when the players are out of cards and only one of them is left alive in the game.
